Services

We offer the following services at Ikigai Informatics:

  1. Genome Survey analyses: Knowledge creation for information sparse organisms

  2. DNASeq with a Reference Genome: Contrast your sample with an available resource using Genome Sequencing

  3. Exome mutation analyses: Explore variation in the Protein Coding regions of a gene in a genome.

  4. Eukaryotic Genome assembly and analyses: Develop resource for a Eukaryotic organism using an assembly by mining features.

  5. Prokaryotic Genome assembly and analyses: Identify features and capture novelty present in the microbe of your preference.

  6. RNASeq for non-model organism - Denovo Analyses: Develop catalogue of transcripts expressed in your species of interest and explore expression patterns.

  7. RNASeq with a Reference Genome/Transcriptome: Exploratory analyses of transcript features and their modulated impact.

  8. smallRNA Analyses (miRNA): Exploratory analyses of micro-RNAs under various conditions and their regulatary impacts on mRNA sequences.
