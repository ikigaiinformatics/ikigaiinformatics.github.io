ANALYSES

Genome Survey

Genome Survey analyses at Ikigai entails extraction of the following information from the datasets using a kmer based approach.

Since Genome Survey is done at 25-30X data (i.e 1Gb genome => 30Gb data), we can analyse the data in the below fashion.

  1. Genome size: Ballpark the size of the genome and mark it against existing literature.

  2. Heterozygosity of genome: Heterozygosity corelates with the presence of allelic variations, which might complicate genome assembly.

  3. Repetitiveness of genome: Know the presence of low-complexity or repetitive motif density in the genome.

Other than the features outlined above, we also offer the following:

  1. Assembly and annotation of organellar genome, with Organelle based phylogeny

  2. Draft assembly and homology based annotation
