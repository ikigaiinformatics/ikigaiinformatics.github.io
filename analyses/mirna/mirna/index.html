ANALYSES

smallRNA Analyses

We offer the following approach to analyse RNASeq data if the reference genome is readily available at public resources like NCBI, ENSEMBL, JGI or others. We prefer having the genome from ENSEMBL due to the downstream integration with biomart.

  1. QC and Read Trimming: Trim adapters and retain reads between 18-30bp.

  2. Alignment to other ncRNAs: ncRNA catalogue from which the smallRNA sequences (precursors or mature) have been removed are used as a bait to remove reads originating from such sequences. Remaining reads are termed as “putative miRNA reads.”

  3. Alignment of miRNA reads: Reads are aligned to the genome, using a short read aligner and later quantified.

  4. Novel miRNA identification: Alignments are also screened and thermodynamically evaluated for the feasibility of secondary structure formation.

  5. Diffential Expression: Identification of Significantly modulated genes.

  6. Target Prediction: miRNA sequences are used to predict their associated transcript sequences to identify impacted genes.

  7. GO and KEGG Pathway analyses of Targetted transcripts: Check the over-representation of Ontologies to see which terms are being significantly impacted. KEGG pathways will also be identified to assess the modulated pathways.
