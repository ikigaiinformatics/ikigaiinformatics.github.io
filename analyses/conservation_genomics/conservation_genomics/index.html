ANALYSES

Conservation Genomics

At Ikigai we offer a special analyses pipeline for creating resources that might help saving flora and fauna from getting extinct.

It is due to our actions, directly or indirectly various factors have arisen that have led to many species getting wiped out. 1 2

Analyses flow at Ikigai:

  1. QC of the datasets to remove low quality data.

  2. Genome Survey analyses to identify various pecularities of the genome.

  3. Alignment to a closer reference if it is available. Otherwise Nuclear and Organellar Genome Assembly can be done (depending on available data) to create a high quality contiguous representation of a genome.

  4. Genome Annotation to identify genomic features like transposable elements, low complexity regions followed by genes and ncRNAs at the end.

  5. Phylogenetic analyses to identify taxonomic placements and study the evolution of genes.

  6. Marker development like Polymorphic SSRs and various minor variations to help identify similar specimens.

  7. Population demographic analyses to help identify when the population crash occured.

  8. Homo/heterozygous fraction of a genome and identify tracts of homozygosity in the genome.
