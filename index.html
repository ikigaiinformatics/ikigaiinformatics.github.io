Welcome to Ikigai Informatics.

Ikigai - A reason for being or purpose to accomplish a task. Our work is to make your mind agile and actualize your passion.

Ikigai is a purely bioinformatics company that specializes in various domains of Next Generation Sequencing data analyses.

Our aim is to help you extract the information hidden in your NGS datasets, mesh with your experiment and provide you with a concrete analyses to give you a peace of mind.

The major motto of Ikigai is Reproducibility: We are well experienced in containerization approaches like Docker and Conda environments to pin down State of the Art Open Source tools used in the analyses.
